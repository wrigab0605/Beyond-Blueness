Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help people who feel like they have nowhere to go. So many people suffer from mental health issues and don't get any treatment or support, or are too scared to admit that they have a problem. Here, people can share their experiences anonymously, read about experiences similar to theirs, and make connections with others who are struggling. Together, we hope to create a tightly-knit support system that values all people regardless of race, gender, orientation, etc.

 

 

Disclaimer: This website does not glorify mental illness.
